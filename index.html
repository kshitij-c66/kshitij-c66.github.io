OUR ETHOS —
ALL ABOUT RAI

The Revolutionary Army in India was born from the ashes of broken promises and the silence of those who chose comfort over courage. We are not an army of violence — we are an army of ideas, truth, and relentless accountability. Formed on 7 February 2026, RAI unites every Indian who believes that a democracy corrupted from within is no democracy at all.

⚔

RAI operates on the Doctrine of Conscious Resistance — a philosophy that distinguishes between blind rebellion and principled dissent. Every citizen has a constitutional duty not merely to vote, but to hold power accountable every single day.

Our symbol — the Torch within the Shield — represents the twin pillars of our identity: protection of the innocent, and illumination of truth. Where darkness of corruption spreads, we carry the flame.

PAGE 03 / GOVERNANCE

PROBLEMS WITH
PRESENT-DAY GOVERNANCE

We do not make accusations lightly. These failures are documented, systematic, and demand the attention of every conscious citizen. RAI identifies them not to despair, but to dismantle and rebuild.

⚖️

INSTITUTIONAL CAPTURE

Investigative agencies, election bodies, and courts increasingly serve the ruling dispensation. Checks and balances become theater when those who check are appointed by those they check.

📰

MEDIA CONSOLIDATION

When conglomerates control national media and their owners owe billions in government contracts, the "free press" is a managed narrative. Citizens deserve journalism free from corporate-political nexus.

💰

OPACITY IN PUBLIC FUNDS

Electoral bond schemes, off-budget borrowings, and opaque procurement processes have made public money invisible. Where public money cannot be tracked, corruption flourishes without consequence.

🏘️

RURAL & URBAN INEQUALITY

Infrastructure, healthcare, and education remain radically unequal across India. Announcements multiply while ground-level delivery stagnates. Statistical sleight-of-hand has replaced genuine poverty alleviation.

🔒

SUPPRESSION OF DISSENT

Sedition laws, UAPA, and contempt charges are disproportionately deployed against journalists, activists, and opposition figures. Criminalizing critique is the hallmark of insecure governance.

🌱

ENVIRONMENTAL NEGLECT

Forests cleared, rivers dammed, air rendered toxic — while impact assessments are diluted and protesting communities are branded anti-national. The earth belongs to future generations too.

◈ RAI'S 10-POINT REFORM MANIFESTO

[01]Independent appointment of constitutional body heads through bipartisan parliamentary committees.
[02]Mandatory real-time public disclosure of all government contracts above ₹10 lakh.
[03]Repeal of colonial-era sedition and public order laws used to silence peaceful dissent.
[04]Universal right to quality healthcare and education enshrined as justiciable fundamental rights.
[05]Independent media regulator free from government composition or control.
[06]Proportional representation reform to ensure legislative seats reflect true vote shares.
[07]Strengthened Lokpal with suo motu powers and guaranteed financial independence.
[08]Climate emergency legislation with binding targets and citizen enforcement rights.
[09]Decentralization of power to panchayats and urban local bodies with commensurate funding.
[10]Constitutional amendment to make corruption by public servants a non-bailable offense.

She gave us the Himalayas as her crown,
